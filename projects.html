Software Projects

My first project undertaken at the Sparta Academy. I spent a weekend planning and a week developing this comedic text adventure game in HTML, CSS and vanilla JavaScript.

COYI

My second project undertaken at the Sparta Academy: a week-long team effort to build a full stack Ruby on Rails website for the Circle of Young Intrapreneurs organisation.

DemoQA Test Framework

My final project at the Sparta Academy. I spent a week in a two-person team building a framework to test the UI of the DemoQA e-commerce website, using Ruby, Cucumber, Rspec, Selenium WebDriver and Capybara.

The Cheese Clicker

I started building this Android application for a Codebar workshop, and worked on it during that workshop and over a weekend afterwards. Similarly to the Cookie Clicker game, a user must tap the cheese in order to accrue points; earning a bonus point for each multiple of ten.

DeliverAid

I collaborated in a multidisciplinary team to win the London Fintech Week Hackathon 2019. Over the course of the hackathon weekend, we crystallised the idea for a tech platform that comprises of an SMS to AI chatbot and global data-driven portal. Our efforts won us the chance to pitch our business to fintech investors at the London Fintech Week conference.

Hacktoberfest 2019 Open Source Website

I decided to spend the majority of my second Hacktoberfest maintaining an open source project aimed specifically at beginners. The responsibility of maintaining this has stretched my skills in Git and front end development, and allowed me to consolidate my knowledge of coding best practices.

Musical Projects

The Mighty John Street Ska Orchestra

For the past three years I have been playing the drums in TMJSSO: a fourteen-piece band covering the music of The Skatalites and Herbie Hancock. We regularly play national festivals such as WOMAD and Boomtown, as well as smaller regional festivals like Stroud Fringe.

The Dirty Nickers

I briefly played the drums in this four-piece experimental punk band while living in Newcastle. Upon moving away, I remotely recorded drum parts in my home studio to be included on their debut album.

Drum Teaching

I have had experience of teaching three students the drumset on a one-to-one basis. I am very keen to share my love of drums and percussion with more people, so don't hesitate to get in touch if you fancy some lessons!
